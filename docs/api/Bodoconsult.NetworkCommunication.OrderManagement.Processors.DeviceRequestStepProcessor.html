Remarks

The execution of a request step consists of two major steps. Step 1 is sending a message to the device. Step 2 is waiting for the answer of the device. The device is normally responsive. A timelag between step1 and step2 may lead to failing device orders due to "lost" device messages. Therefore we start step 2 before step 1 is fired.
